## What

Cal Newport is a computer science professor at Georgetown University and the author of several books on productivity, focus, and the workplace. He writes the *Study Hacks* blog and the *Deep Questions* podcast.

## Why He Matters Here

Newport's work is the canonical modern reference on focus as a trainable skill rather than a personality trait. His framings (deep vs. shallow, attention residue, scheduling philosophies) keep showing up across sources, so he gets an entity page rather than being mentioned ad-hoc in each.
